## How to apply

When generating resume, LinkedIn, or website copy, describe this tier honestly: "experimented with," "built prototypes using," "hands-on with" — not "led production AI systems" or "shipped at scale." If production-scale AI experience is gained later, this document is the first place to update, before any generated artifact reflects it.
