# Content Strategy

## Primary themes

- AI Product Engineering
- Founder CTO
- Architecture
- Systems Thinking
- Building SaaS
- Engineering Leadership
- Azure
- AI Experiments
- Product Strategy
- Technology Evolution

## What to avoid

Avoid becoming another AI influencer — content that's mostly hot takes on AI news rather than evidence of building.

## What to prefer instead

- Building in public
- Sharing experiments
- Architecture notes
- Lessons learned
- Case studies

## How to apply

Every piece of content (newsletter, talk, article) should trace back to something actually built or learned — see [build-learn-share-repeat.md](../philosophy/build-learn-share-repeat.md). If a content idea doesn't correspond to a real build or experiment recorded somewhere in this repository, it's the wrong kind of content for this strategy, regardless of how topical it is.
