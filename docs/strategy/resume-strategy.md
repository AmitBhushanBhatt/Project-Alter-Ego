# Resume Strategy

## What the resume must communicate

- Founder CTO
- AI-first SaaS Architect
- Startup CTO
- Technology Executive
- Builder

These come directly from [positioning.md](../identity/positioning.md) — this doc adds no new facts, only rules for how the resume should use them.

## What to avoid

- Do not optimize the resume for Principal Engineer, Staff Engineer, Distinguished Engineer, or VP Engineering roles. See the guardrails in [positioning.md](../identity/positioning.md#positioning-guardrails).
- Do not inflate AI expertise. Describe it exactly as framed in [ai-experience.md](../career/ai-experience.md) — experimental/hands-on, not production-scale.
- Add quantified achievements only when factual. No estimated or rounded-up numbers presented as measured facts.

## How to apply

When generating a resume from this repository: pull positioning language from [identity](../identity/), era structure from [eras.md](../career/eras.md), leadership claims from [leadership.md](../career/leadership.md), and AI framing from [ai-experience.md](../career/ai-experience.md) — verbatim in spirit, not reworded into something bolder. If a resume draft says something the source domains don't support, fix the source domain first (per [ai-governance.md](../knowledge/ai-governance.md)) or cut the claim.
